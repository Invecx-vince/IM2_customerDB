About the Shop

Sweet Guilty Pleasures started out as a small dream by the "Petit Chef", Fae Therese C. Del Rosario, after watching her grandmother's blooming Fresh Lumpia business. And from that dream, grew a desire to make it come true. After two years of training from an international culinary school,

Sweet Guilty Pleasures Cebu has made its name, both online and on Cebu's culinary market. With a wide array of pastries for you to customize, we promise to offer you sweets that are too good to resist, both for the eyes, and the appetite.

The Mission

"To provide attractive and delicious treats to customers and pets, with the best quality."


The Vision

"Provide treats and sweets that are edible and delightful for anyone and everyone."
